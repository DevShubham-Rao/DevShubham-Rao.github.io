Our Menu

Starters & Salads

  • Heirloom Tomato & Basil Bruschetta – Served with house-made extra virgin olive oil and aged balsamic reduction, made from organic Italian grapes.
  • Quinoa & Kale Salad – Tossed with roasted seasonal vegetables, toasted almonds, and a zesty lemon-tahini dressing sourced from local sesame farms.
  • Roasted Beet & Citrus Salad – Finished with toasted walnuts, fresh mint, and a drizzle of honey from our local apiary partners.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Portobello Mushroom Burger – Marinated in house-made herbs and garlic, topped with creamy avocado spread and caramelized onions on a whole grain bun baked fresh daily.
  • Butternut Squash Risotto – Creamy Arborio rice cooked slowly with vegetable broth, fresh sage, and toasted pumpkin seeds harvested from nearby farms.
  • Vegetable Stir-Fry – A colorful medley of seasonal veggies sautéed with tamari, fresh ginger, and garlic, served over organic brown rice.

Preparation Highlights

Our chefs use a combination of culinary techniques designed to retain nutrients while maximizing flavor. From sous-vide vegetable medleys to freshly made sauces and dressings, every plate showcases careful preparation.

We emphasize whole foods and natural seasonings. Our kitchen avoids frying and processed additives, relying on fermentation, roasting, and steaming to elevate our dishes.

Gluten-free, vegan, and allergen-friendly options are available, ensuring that everyone can enjoy our food with confidence.

Carry-Out & Dine-In

Enjoy our meals in the cozy atmosphere of Verdant Kitchen or order online for quick, convenient carry-out options. All packaging is compostable, made from plant-based materials to reduce waste.

We encourage sustainable choices by offering discounts for bringing reusable containers and bags.
