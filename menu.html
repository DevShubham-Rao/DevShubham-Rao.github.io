Our Menu

Starters & Salads

  • Heirloom Tomato & Basil Bruschetta – Served with house-made extra virgin olive oil and aged balsamic reduction, made from organic Italian grapes.
  • Quinoa & Kale Salad – Tossed with roasted seasonal vegetables, toasted almonds, and a zesty lemon-tahini dressing sourced from local sesame farms.
  • Roasted Beet & Citrus Salad – Finished with toasted walnuts, fresh mint, and a drizzle of honey from our local apiary partners.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Portobello Mushroom Burger – Marinated in house-made herbs and garlic, topped with creamy avocado spread and caramelized onions on a whole grain bun baked fresh daily.
  • Butternut Squash Risotto – Creamy Arborio rice cooked slowly with vegetable broth, fresh sage, and toasted pumpkin seeds harvested from nearby farms.
  • Vegetable Stir-Fry – A colorful medley of seasonal veggies sautéed with tamari, fresh ginger, and garlic, served over organic brown rice.

Desserts

  • Coconut & Mango Sorbet – A refreshing, dairy-free chilled dessert bursting with tropical flavor.
  • Chocolate Avocado Mousse – Creamy and rich, naturally sweetened with dates and a hint of vanilla.

Preparation Highlights

Our chefs use a combination of culinary techniques designed to retain nutrients while maximizing flavor. From sous-vide vegetable medleys to freshly made sauces and dressings, every plate showcases the best of our kitchen.

We emphasize whole foods and natural seasonings. Our kitchen avoids frying and processed additives, relying on techniques like fermentation, roasting, and steaming to bring out the natural goodness in every ingredient.

Gluten-free, vegan, and allergen-friendly options are always available so that everyone can enjoy our food with confidence.

Carry-Out & Dine-In

Enjoy our meals in the cozy atmosphere of Verdant Kitchen or opt for our quick, convenient carry-out options. All of our packaging is compostable and made from plant-based materials to reduce waste.

We also offer discounts for customers who bring their own reusable containers and bags.
